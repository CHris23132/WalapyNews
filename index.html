Walapy - the news written for you.

Walapy is a modern news app that takes advantage of big data and artificial intelligence.

Walapy uses top data pipelines and real time news api to fetch and retrieve data for location specific news reporting. Walapy uses ai to take the retrieved news data and feed the data into a OpenAI ChatGPT query to create a highly reliable custom news article for the user based on their location and categories.
