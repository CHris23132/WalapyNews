Privacy Policy for Walapy

Last updated: 2024

Introduction

Walapy is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the non-collection of personal data.

No Data Collection

Walapy does not collect, store, or process any personal data from its users. Our service functions by:

  • Processing search queries in real-time without storing them
  • Accessing public news APIs to retrieve relevant news content
  • Using OpenAI's ChatGPT to summarize news content
  • Delivering results directly to users without saving any information

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • News APIs for content retrieval
  • OpenAI's ChatGPT for news summarization

These services are used solely for processing immediate requests and do not store user data on our behalf.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our services evolve. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please visit our Contact page.
